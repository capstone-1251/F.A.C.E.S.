About Us

The Filipino Academic and Cultural Educators Society is a non-profit organization that aims to promote and preserve the Filipino culture and heritage through education, research, and advocacy. F.A.C.E.S will comprise of students, teachers, researchers and cultural educators who share a common passion for enrichment, inclusion and diversity of the Filipino culture.

The organization was formed during the First Filipino Language and Culture Conference on October 15-16, 2022 at the Philippine Cultural Centre Foundation. The conference was a joint effort from the Philippine Consulate in Calgary led by Consul General Zaldy Patron and the National Commission for Culture and Arts (NCCA) Philippines with the coordination from the two community based organization.
