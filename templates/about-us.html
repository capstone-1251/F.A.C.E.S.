Our Story

The Filipino Academic and Cultural Educators Society of or F.A.C.E.S. was formed during the First Filipino Language and Culture Conference on October 15-16, 2022 at the Philippine Cultural Centre Foundation. The conference was a joint effort from the Philippine Consulate in Calgary led by Consul General Zaldy Patron and the National Commission for Culture and Arts (NCCA) Philippines with the coordination from the two community based organization; Filipino Language and Culture schools in Edmonton (Filipino Canadian Saranay Association of Alberta) and Calgary (Philippine Cultural Centre Foundation).

Leaders from the different Filipino organizations were formed to lead and advocate for the Filipino Language and Culture Education in Alberta and Saskatchewan. On September 2023, the leaders formed from the First Filipino Language and Culture conference formally incorporated the group and the Filipino Academic and Cultural Educators Society of F.A.C.E.S. was created.

Our Mission

The Filipino Academic and Cultural Educators Society is a non-profit organization that aims to promote and preserve the Filipino culture and heritage through education, research, and advocacy.

Our Community

F.A.C.E.S will comprise of students, teachers, researchers and cultural educators who share a common passion for enrichment, inclusion and diversity of the Filipino culture.
